Programming living symbioses

AstroBiota is pioneering Symbiology Engineering — the science of programming living systems and their symbioses. Our breakthrough concept, A‑temporal Biology, reveals life stages that do not persist in nature, opening new frontiers in how biology can be preserved, studied, and designed.

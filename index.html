What this is

AstroBiota is an independent biological observatory focused on capturing living systems across field, culture, and time.

The archive preserves photographs, strains, reports, and observational records as stable scientific memory. The research layer develops the writing, field reports, and concepts that emerge from those systems.

The long-term direction is an autonomous observatory stack: living systems, preserved artifacts, and machine reasoning systems working together inside one reliable observation environment.

Trace Invariance Logic extends this work into machine reasoning systems by structuring observation around inspectable biological artifacts. See Runtime.

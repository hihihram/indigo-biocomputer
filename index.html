About AstroBiota

AstroBiota is an independent biological observatory focused on capturing and preserving real biological systems as they evolve across field, culture, and time.

It treats visible trace as primary data: structures, pigments, growth patterns, and biological artifacts that remain inspectable after change has occurred.

Trace Invariance Logic extends this work into machine reasoning systems by structuring observation around inspectable artifacts. See Runtime.

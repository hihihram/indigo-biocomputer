About

AstroBiota is pioneering Symbiology Engineering — the science of programming living systems and their symbioses. Our breakthrough, A‑temporal Biology, reveals life stages that don’t persist in nature, opening new frontiers in how biology can be preserved, studied, and designed.

We are building a platform for biobanking and engineering resilient symbioses — preparing biology for conservation on Earth and for adaptation to environments beyond our planet.

An independent autonomous observatory

AstroBiota exists to document living systems that hold memory in physical form. Its focus is not just on what organisms are, but on what they record through time.

This work treats trace as primary scientific material: field specimens, cultures, phenotypes, and structural residues that remain inspectable after change has occurred.

The long-term direction is an autonomous observatory capable of collecting artifact-anchored records, structuring observation, and preserving discovery before it is compressed into interpretation.

AstroBiota is research-led, archive-backed, and intentionally upstream. Some outcomes may become tools, reports, or products; others may remain foundational observation infrastructure.
